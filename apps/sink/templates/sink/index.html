About

Use U-Boat to easily download subtitles in batch for your series and movies, by simply selecting the files on your hard disk. Currently only OpenSubtitles.org is supported. You will need an account there to use this application.

We are still in a beta phase. Some unforeseen issues might pop up. Please let us know if you find bugs or things to improve through the channels in the CONTACT section.
